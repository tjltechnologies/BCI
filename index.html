Our Products

We specialize in a wide variety of Ball Clay and Clay Lumps

Washed Ball Clay

A purified ball clay variety that has been thoroughly washed to remove impurities and excess fines. It offers high plasticity and purity, making it ideal for quality ceramic production.

High MoR Ball Clay

A strong ball clay type with a high modulus of rupture, providing enhanced mechanical strength. Perfect for structural ceramics and applications requiring durable material properties.

High MoR Processed Ball Clay

Ball clay specially processed to achieve a high modulus of rupture, ensuring superior strength and reliability. Suitable for high-stress ceramic products demanding robust performance.

Levigated China Clay

China clay refined through levigation, resulting in a fine and uniform particle size with high purity. Ideal for fine ceramics, porcelain, and applications requiring consistent material quality.

China Clay Lumps

Natural, unprocessed chunks of china clay retaining their original mineral composition. Used as a raw material for further refinement or in bulk ceramic manufacturing.

Hydrous Clay

Clay minerals containing bound water, providing excellent plasticity and workability. Widely used in ceramics and industrial applications for their moisture-related properties.
